The Last Psyop is an in-person, weekend long event in Austin TX - Friday September 4th, @ 5pm -> Sunday September 7th, 6pm.

We're inviting folks who:

  1. have skill at navigating information warfare, and
  2. are willing to play a game where all their actions documented.
Players at the event compete in teams to "capture territory" in the information landscape, while the world watches, analyzes, and learns from their movements.

We also welcome researchers, analysts, and those who can create visualizations of the data generated by the players as the game unfolds.

See GitHub for backstage planning & the discussion: https://github.com/Kat-Stack/netwar-con
